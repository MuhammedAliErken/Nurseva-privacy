Welcome to Nurseva

Nurseva is a smart application designed to help mothers control and monitor their breast pumps efficiently. We are committed to providing a secure, intuitive experience while respecting your privacy and data.

Your privacy matters

We collect only the minimum information required to deliver and improve the core features of the app. Your health and pumping data stay on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync or share them.

Key app capabilities

  • Bluetooth control of your wearable breast pump
  • Session tracking (duration, patterns, and comfort settings)
  • Optional notes and records to support your feeding routine
  • Device management and pairing for multiple pumps
  • Privacy-first approach with transparent data usage

About our Privacy Policy

The full Privacy Policy explains in detail:

  • What information we collect (device data, usage data, optional account data)
  • How we use and protect your information
  • When limited third-party services may be involved
  • Your rights to access, correct, or delete your data

To read the full legal text, please use the “Privacy Policy” button above.

At a glance

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Bluetooth is used only to connect and control your pump.
  • Locally stored data is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Optional cloud or analytics tools follow strict privacy standards.
