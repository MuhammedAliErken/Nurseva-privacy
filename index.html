Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22 November 2025

Nurseva (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Nurseva mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App and the Nurseva wearable breast pump.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device & App Information

We may collect technical data such as:

  • Device model, operating system, and version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Bluetooth connection status and performance data

1.2 Pump Usage Data

To improve your pumping experience, the App may collect:

  • Session duration
  • Pump mode and intensity settings
  • Connection logs for troubleshooting

1.3 Optional User Data

If you choose to create an account or back up your data, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Profile preferences
  • Manually entered notes or logs

We do not collect any health data without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s core features
  • Enable Bluetooth communication with the pump
  • Show logs and personalized pumping insights
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Enhance overall user experience

We never sell your data.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

If you delete the App, any locally stored data is removed from your device. If you created an account, you may request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party tools such as:

  • Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud backup services (optional)

These services follow their own privacy policies.

5. Bluetooth Permissions

The App requires Bluetooth access to:

  • Detect your Nurseva pump
  • Connect and control the pump
  • Track session activity

Bluetooth data is used only for device pairing and control.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your account data
  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Stop using the App at any time

To make a request, contact us at: support@nursevapp.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@nursevapp.com

This Privacy Policy applies to the Nurseva mobile application and the Nurseva wearable breast pump.
