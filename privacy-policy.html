Welcome to Nurseva. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the Nurseva app:

  • Baby Information: Name, date of birth, gender, and photos (optional)
  • Pumping Records: Session duration, milk volume, pumping times, and notes
  • Feeding Records: Feeding type (breast milk, formula, mixed), amounts, and times
  • Device Information: Bluetooth device names and connection history
  • Preferences: App settings, language preferences, and unit preferences (ml/oz)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session frequency, and app performance data
  • Bluetooth Data: Nearby Bluetooth devices for pairing with your breast pump
⚠️ Important: We do NOT collect your precise geolocation data. Bluetooth scanning on Android 11 and below requires location permission, but we never access or store your location information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To enable Bluetooth connectivity with your breast pump and track pumping/feeding sessions
  • Data Storage: To save your pumping and feeding records locally on your device
  • User Experience: To personalize your experience and remember your preferences
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your health and pumping data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal health information on remote servers. This means:

  • Your pumping records stay on your phone
  • Your baby's information remains private and local
  • No cloud synchronization of health data
  • You maintain full control over your data

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data on your device
  • Secure Bluetooth communication protocols
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • No transmission of health data to third parties

4. Permissions We Request

Nurseva requests the following permissions to function properly:

4.1 Bluetooth Permissions (Required)

  • BLUETOOTH_SCAN: To scan for and discover your breast pump device
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT: To connect and communicate with your breast pump
  • Purpose: Essential for controlling your breast pump via Bluetooth

4.2 Location Permission (Android 11 and below only)

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: Required by Android for Bluetooth scanning on older versions
  • Important: We never access or store your actual location data
  • Note: This permission is NOT required on Android 12 and above

4.3 Storage Permission (Optional)

  • Purpose: To save and load baby photos (if you choose to add them)
  • Note: You can use the app without granting this permission

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • App Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

6. Third-Party Services

Nurseva may use third-party services for app functionality:

6.1 Analytics (if applicable)

We may use analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance. These services collect:

  • Device information and app version
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and performance data
Note: Analytics services do NOT have access to your health data, pumping records, or baby information.

7. Children's Privacy

Nurseva is intended for use by parents and caregivers (18+ years old). While the app collects information about babies for record-keeping purposes, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data entry is performed by adult users.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete specific records or clear all data through app settings
  • Export: Export your data for backup purposes (feature may vary)
  • Permissions: Revoke app permissions at any time through device settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

9. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You can delete specific records or all data at any time through the app settings. When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed.

10. International Data Transfers

Since all your health and pumping data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. The app can be used anywhere in the world without data leaving your device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
